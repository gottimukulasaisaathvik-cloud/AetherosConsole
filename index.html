Welcome to Aetheros Console

Aetheros is a comprehensive entry to the National Space Society (NSS) Gerard K. O'Neill Space Settlement Design Competition. Our console provides advanced analytics and real-time data visualization for space settlement design parameters.

This platform enables researchers and designers to analyze critical metrics for a sustainable, rotating torus space habitat, including life support systems, structural parameters, and resource management data.

About the Aetheros Design

Aetheros envisions a rotating torus configuration featuring:

  • Advanced radiation shielding for long-term habitability
  • Artificial gravity through rotation at optimal parameters
  • Sustainable agricultural and manufacturing systems
  • Multi-zone habitat design for diverse activities
  • Robust communication systems for Earth connectivity
  • Self-sufficient life support and resource cycles
